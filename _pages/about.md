Welcome to my website! I hold a Ph.D. in Political Science from Claremont Graduate University, specializing in Political Theory and American Politics. My research interests lie in American political thought and institutions, and political psychology and race and ethnic politics. I am interested in the American Founding, American slavery, representation, polarization, populism, and rhetoric. My theoretical interests include the works of the American Founders, Alexis de Tocqueville, Abraham Lincoln, and Frederick Douglass. I also use both administrative and public opinion data to evaluate American democracy.
